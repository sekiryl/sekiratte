Usage Recommendations

This palette embodies the somber, atmospheric mood of Ashina with sudden bursts of crimson (combat) and gold (rewards). Below are recommended pairings for different UI contexts and themes.

Combat UI Elements

Use base + text + red for critical combat elements. This combination creates high contrast for important information during battle, with the blood red accent drawing immediate attention.

Interactive Items

Pair surface0 with yellow and green for interactive items and consumables. The temple wood background complements the sen coins and divine grass colors.

Mystical Themes

Combine blue and lavender for mystical/divine themes. These hues evoke the waters of Fountainhead Palace and the otherworldly spirits.
